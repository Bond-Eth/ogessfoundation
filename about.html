About OGE's Foundation

OGE’s Foundation is a community-driven non-profit organisation dedicated to improving the lives of vulnerable children and families through food security, education, and health interventions. Since our founding, we've focused on sustainable, dignity-preserving support — creating long-term change in communities across our region.

Our story

OGE was founded in the year 2023 by Ogechi Ernestina Igboke, driven by a passion of giving and love for humanity. Our journey began with a simple idea: By providing healthy food for the malnourished children with my cooking skills. Today, we're a team of dedicated professionals committed to ensuring that we provide these children with the best quality education, healthy food and water and empowerment programs.

Our mission

To feed, educate and empower children and vulnerable families, enabling them to build brighter futures.
